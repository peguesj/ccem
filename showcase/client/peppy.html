Scope Statement
Peppy is a physician-supervised DTC telehealth platform for peptide compounding therapy. It serves health-optimized adults seeking evidence-based optimization through curated peptide stacks (Pepp Rallies), licensed physician consultations (Pepp Talks), and ongoing protocol management. Peppy does not dispense unregulated peptides, operate as a pharmacy, provide prescriptions without physician review, or handle DEA-scheduled compounds directly. The platform delivers a compelling subscription experience while maintaining full 503A and HIPAA compliance as the ceiling, not the floor.
